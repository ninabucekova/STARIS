I WOULD STILL BE MARRIED AND HOPEFULLY MY DAUGHTER WOULD BE SETTLED DOWN WITH CHILDREN OF HER OWN, I WOULD STILL BE FIT ENOUGH TO ENJOY HOLIDAYS AND TRAVEL.
