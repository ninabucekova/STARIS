BACK IN ENGLAND, ENJOYING OUR DAYS OFF WORK, IN THE GARDEN OR SOCIALISING WITH FAMILY AND FRIENDS, TRIPS TO THE EAST COAST TO VISIT MUM AND DAD, TAKE THEM SHOPPING, MAYBE STILL HAVE D.I.Y.
