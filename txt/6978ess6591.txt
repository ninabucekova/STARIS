I STILL CYCLE 100 MILES A WEEK AND AM LOOKING FORWARD TO CYCLING MORE, I AM SPENDING MORE TIME SKIING IN THE ALPS AND AM LOOKING FORWARD TO SPENDING WHOLE WINTERS THERE WHEN MY DAUGHTER IS AT UNI.
